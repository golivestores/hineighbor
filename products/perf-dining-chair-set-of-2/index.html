304 stainless steel is a widely recognized alloy known for its exceptional strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion. It’s the same grade trusted in commercial kitchens, architectural fixtures, and high-end outdoor furniture. Most outdoor furniture on the market is made from mild steel, which is more prone to corrosion, wear, and early replacement. 304 stainless, on the other hand, holds up beautifully in all kinds of weather, even near the coast. In short: professional-grade performance with patio-ready ease.
